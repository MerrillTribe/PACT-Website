# Join PACT

The **Provo Auxiliary Communications Team (PACT)** is a volunteer group that supports emergency communications during incidents, events, and disasters.

PACT members assist public safety agencies by providing trained radio operators who can help maintain communications when normal systems are overloaded or unavailable.

---

## Who Should Join

PACT may be a good fit if you:

- Are interested in amateur radio
- Want to serve the community
- Are willing to train and participate in exercises
- Can assist during community events or emergencies

No prior emergency communications experience is required.

---

## Requirements

Typical expectations for PACT members include:

- Amateur Radio License (Technician or higher recommended)
- Participation in training exercises
- Ability to deploy when requested
- Commitment to professionalism during operations
