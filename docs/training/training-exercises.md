# Training Exercises & Events

## Provo Prepared Emergency Communications Exercise

### Event Overview

This exercise prepares Provo's neighborhoods for staying connected when it matters most.

This Emergency Communications Exercise is a starting point for building a reliable, community-based communication plan. During emergencies, traditional systems like cell and internet services can become limited or unavailable. This exercise provides an opportunity for residents practice a neighborhood-based communication plan. You will learn how to work with your neighbors to communicate critical information with city officials during a crisis.

This exercise provides clear guidance and practical tools to help strengthen your neighborhood readiness. By participating, you help create a more resilient community that can respond quickly, share accurate information, and support one another during unexpected events.

Get involved, stay informed, and help your neighborhood be prepared.
