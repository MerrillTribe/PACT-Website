# Meetings

## Interface Meetings
An Interface Meeting happens every quarter. These meetings are a platform for the PACT Leadership team to sharing information, discuss upcoming operations, and conducting training. These meetings help ensure that all members are prepared and familiar with their fellow PACT members. By attending the Interface Meetings, you contribute to the strength and readiness of PACT. Your involvement is crucial in maintaining an effective organization. 

We look forward to seeing you at our next Interface Meeting!
