## Deployment Checklist

<details>
  <summary><strong>Deployment Checklist</strong></summary>

- [ ] Radio equipment  
- [ ] Printed reporting forms  
- [ ] Notebook and pen  
- [ ] Appropriate clothing and water  
- [ ] Backup power source, if possible  
- [ ] PACT vest, if issued or available for planned exercises  

</details>

    <details>
      <summary>View details</summary>

      <ul>
        <li>Program all PACT frequencies before deployment.</li>
        <li>Carry at least one spare battery if possible.</li>
        <li>Use a speaker mic or earpiece in noisy locations.</li>
        <li>Keep transmissions short and professional.</li>
      </ul>
    </details>
  </div>

  <div className="equipment-card">
    <img
      src="/img/equipment/power-supply.png"
      alt="Power Supply"
      className="equipment-card-icon"
    />

    <h3>Power Supply</h3>

    <p>
      Backup power helps maintain communications if commercial power is unavailable.
    </p>

    <details>
      <summary>View details</summary>

      <ul>
        <li>Bring charged battery packs when possible.</li>
        <li>Consider Anderson Powerpole compatibility.</li>
        <li>Keep charging cables with your radio equipment.</li>
        <li>Do not rely on ACC emergency power being available.</li>
      </ul>
    </details>
  </div>

</div>
